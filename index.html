GAME RULES:

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls the two dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • AND, if the players rolls two consecutives 6, all his TOTAL score gets lost!
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game (you can change the winning score
